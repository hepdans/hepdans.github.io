I started dancing with Tango. I then decided that I wanted something more energetic and fun, so I shifted to Salsa and Bachata. I looked for numerous dancing schools before finally landing in Hep Dans thanks to a friend’s advice. After an introductory lesson I realized that Hep Dans was it for me. The way Hande teaches us (always very detailed and shows even the smallest steps) and how she always pays attention to everyone creates a welcoming environment, and I just love it. She knows how to motivate us. To spend more time at the school I joined intermediate and advances classes. And I never miss Sunday practices.

Birthdays are never celebrated without a proper birthday cake! And even if there isn’t anything to celebrate, we always go out at nights. Hande always invites us all. This way she creates a very social and friendly environment for all of us to enjoy. I have met a lot of amazing people here and I have attended many events with them. Now, I always have a place to go and have fun at weekends and nights. Thus, you know many people at places you go to and they know you, too! You can never feel alone at Latin dance nights.

Even if I have very busy workdays, am going through stressful times or feel down, I always attend our classes. Because as soon as you hear the music and start dancing, you forget about all the negativity. Since you focus only on what you are doing, nothing else matters anymore. I can blow off some steam while I am dancing and leave my daily business life behind. Salsa is the best way to go because it involves exciting moves and music.

I am nowhere near being talented. But thanks to Hande and my personal efforts, I think I can dance a lot better now. I have made great progress in Salsa from the very basics to different combinations, to music appreciation and to styling. Salsa and Bachata have many cool tricks. As you learn them the way you dance becomes more elegant.

Whoever wants to learn or try to learn how to dance today should try it at Hep Dans. It is best place to take up dancing or take up from where you once left off.
